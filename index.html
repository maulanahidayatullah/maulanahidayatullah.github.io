ABOUT ME

Hello! I’m Maulana, a backend developer with a background in Software Engineering from Indramayu State Polytechnic. I specialize in building RESTful APIs, integrating third-party services, and deploying scalable backend systems using Express.js, Spring Boot, Laravel, and .NET. My experience includes projects such as facial recognition attendance systems, visitor management with access control integration, EV charger platforms (OCPP), and custom API integrations like WhatsApp OTP, SnapShell OCR, and Xendit payment gateway. I’m also skilled in PostgreSQL, Linux server setup, and system deployment on client environments. As a fast learner and problem solver, I’m committed to continuous improvement and delivering reliable backend solutions in team-driven environments.
